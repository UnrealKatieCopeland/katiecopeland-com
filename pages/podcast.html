What to Expect

The Void, Explained

The "avoidable void" is the gap between what the ADA requires and what courts actually deliver. It's the accommodation request that disappears into silence. The ADA coordinator who doesn't exist. The self-evaluation that was never completed. The interactive process that never began.

This podcast names that gap, examines its causes, and — most importantly — offers practical tools for closing it. Because the void isn't inevitable. It's avoidable. And that's the point.
