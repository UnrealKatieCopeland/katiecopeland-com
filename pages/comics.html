Meet Ruby & Moon

Ruby is a Blenheim Cavalier King Charles Spaniel who issues Royal Decrees about disability rights from her velvet cushion. Moon is a tricolor Cavalier who holds the clipboard. Together, they make the law accessible — one decree at a time.

Through humor and heart, the Royal Decrees translate complex legal obligations under ADA Title II into clear, shareable guidance that anyone can understand. Because the law shouldn't require a law degree to follow.

About the Series

Why Comics?

The Royal Decrees were born from a simple observation: the law is already on the side of people with disabilities. The problem isn't the law — it's that the people who need to follow it can't find it, can't read it, or can't remember it when it matters.

Ruby and Moon solve that problem. Each decree takes a specific legal obligation under ADA Title II and translates it into a format that's visually clear, emotionally engaging, and impossible to forget. They're designed to be printed, shared, posted in courthouses, and used in CLE presentations.

Because when a Cavalier King Charles Spaniel wearing a crown can explain your legal obligations more clearly than your compliance manual, something needs to change.
